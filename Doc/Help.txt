Hello! We are the SLEDS app team that is currently developing Omniapp. Omniapp is 
an application that allows users to both find and submit mobile applications that runs on any platform
whether it be android, apple, or windows just to name a few. Users can both comment and rate any applications
in our database simply by logging in to their Omniapp account. Users also have the ability to submit an
application to our website on which others can rate and comment.
